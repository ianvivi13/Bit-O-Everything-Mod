Bit O' Everything

The Bit O' Everything mod adds many things to minecraft - new mobs, structures, materials, biomes, dimensions, magic, and much much more!

This mod was developed by: ianvivi13 & UnicycleUnicorn

Skulk Sensor

Block that detects sounds. If a sound comes from the player, it may trigger a shrieker to summon the warden

Powdered Snow

Snow that the player can fall into a freeze if not wearing leather boots

Dragon Egg

Beacon

Has a large light beam that gives the player beneficial status effects:
  • Regeneration
  • Haste
  • Speed
  • Jump Boost
  • Resistance
  • Strength
